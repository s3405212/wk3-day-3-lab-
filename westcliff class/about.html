About Us

Central Park Carriage Rides has been part of New York City since 1983. We offer relaxing horse-drawn carriage rides through Central Park, giving visitors a simple and enjoyable way to experience the park.

Our drivers are friendly and knowledgeable, and they’ll share some fun facts and history along the way — so it’s not just a ride, but also a little tour.
