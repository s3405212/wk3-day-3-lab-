Welcome

Experience Central Park in a different way — with a relaxing horse-drawn carriage ride through some of the most beautiful spots in the city.

From iconic landmarks like Bethesda Fountain to quiet paths by the lake, each ride offers a calm and scenic escape from the busy streets of New York.

Rides are available year-round, including cozy winter rides through snow-covered paths. Perfect for couples, families, or anyone just wanting to slow down and enjoy the moment.
